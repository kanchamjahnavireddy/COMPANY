Text and Content Tags

  • h1 to h6 tags: Define HTML headings, with h1 tag being the largest/most important and h6 tag the smallest.
  • p tag: Defines a paragraph of text.
  • br tag: Inserts a single line break without starting a new paragraph (self-closing tag).
  • hr tag: Inserts a horizontal line across the page, often used to separate sections of content (self-closing tag).
